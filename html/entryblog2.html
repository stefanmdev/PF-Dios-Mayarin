Great British Beer Festival 2022

He Great British Beer Festival in Britain is held, once a year, in August. The festival that lasts for five days promises a continuous fiesta with diverse beers’ producers and various kinds of local and foreign beers ciders, Perries, etc… I addition the visitors can enjoy traditional pub games, live music, traditional snacks and guided beer tasting. This is a huge event that attracts thousands of celebrators each year. The festival’s program, lineup information and the admission fee cost are present in the festival’s website.
